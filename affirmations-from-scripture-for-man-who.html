I Am Strong and Purposeful

Affirmations from Scripture for the Man Who Walks in His Power

This collection of Bible-based affirmations is for the man who knows his worth — the one who embraces his divine purpose and stands firm in his calling. Each verse, drawn from the Bible in Basic English (BBE), has been transformed into a first-person declaration of truth. These affirmations are not just words, but principles to live by. Speak them daily, and let them shape your mindset into one of confidence, strength, and clarity of purpose.

1. I am who I am, made in the image of God.

“And God said to Moses, I am what I am.”
— Exodus 3:14

2. I stand firm, knowing that I am God's instrument.

“Be strong and of good courage, for the Lord your God is with you wherever you go.”
— Joshua 1:9

3. The Lord is my shepherd; I lack nothing.

“The Lord is my shepherd; I will not want.”
— Psalm 23:1

4. I am made strong in the Lord, for He is my strength.

“The Lord is my strength and my shield; my heart trusted in him, and I am helped.”
— Psalm 28:7

5. Whatever I ask in faith, I believe I have received.

“Whatever you make a request for in prayer, have faith that it has been given to you, and you will have it.”
— Mark 11:24

6. I speak to the mountain, and it must move.

“If you have faith as a grain of mustard seed, you will say to this mountain, 'Remove hence to yonder place,' and it shall remove.”
— Matthew 17:20

7. I am a man of courage, and I fear nothing.

“Be strong and of good courage, and do not be afraid.”
— Deuteronomy 31:6

8. The Lord’s plans for me are full of hope and a future.

“For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, says the Lord, thoughts of peace and not of evil, to give you an expected end.”
— Jeremiah 29:11

9. I am the head, and not the tail; I lead with wisdom.

“You will be above only, and you will not be beneath.”
— Deuteronomy 28:13

10. I am a new creation in Christ, old things have passed away.

“Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new.”
— 2 Corinthians 5:17

11. I am called, chosen, and equipped for greatness.

“You are a chosen generation, a royal priesthood, a holy nation, a peculiar people.”
— 1 Peter 2:9

12. I walk by faith, and not by sight.

“For we walk by faith, not by sight.”
— 2 Corinthians 5:7

13. I am more than a conqueror through Him who loves me.

“In all these things, we are more than conquerors through him that loved us.”
— Romans 8:37

14. The joy of the Lord is my strength.

“The joy of the Lord is your strength.”
— Nehemiah 8:10

15. I am the warrior who rises up in faith and purpose.

“I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.”
— Philippians 4:13

Speak these words aloud every day. Let them permeate your being, shaping your mind and actions into the man of strength, purpose, and conviction you were always meant to be. You are not just waiting for fulfilment — you are already walking in it.
