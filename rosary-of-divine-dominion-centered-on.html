Rosary of Divine Dominion: Centered on the Law of Assumption

Masculine, Will-Based, BBE Version. A Neville Goddard-inspired meditative structure

Centred on conscious direction, faith in action, and inner authority.

Opening (Introductory Invocation)

At the Cross: I Am the Cause
“See now that I, even I, am he; there is no god but me: I put to death and I give life.” — Deuteronomy 32:39

First Bead: Assumption as Power

“Say not in your heart, Who will go up to heaven?… But what does it say? The word is near you, in your mouth and in your heart.” — Romans 10:6-8

Next Three Beads (Establish Inner Authority)

“Be strong and of good courage… for the Lord your God is with you wherever you go.” — Joshua 1:9
“My strength is in quiet watching for God.” — Lamentations 3:26
“Have I not said to you, Be strong and full of courage?” — Joshua 1:9

Before First Decade (Align the Mind)

“As a man thinks in his heart, so is he.” — Proverbs 23:7

Five Decades: The Firm Will of Fulfilment

Each decade includes a central verse (the “Will Declaration”) and ten affirming verses (“Steps”), drawing out the masculine themes of initiation, confidence, structure, and dominion.

Decade 1: I Am the Doer of the Word

Will Declaration:
“Be doers of the word and not only hearers.” — James 1:22

Step (Repeat 10 times):
“I will not be moved, because the Lord is at my right hand.” — Psalm 16:8

Decade 2: I Am the Sent One

Will Declaration:
“The spirit of the Lord is on me, because I have been marked out by him.” — Luke 4:18

Step (Repeat 10 times):
“You are my servant; I have taken you, and not let you go.” — Isaiah 41:9

Decade 3: I Command My Day

Will Declaration:
“You will make your prayer to him, and he will give ear to you… you will give an order, and it will be done.” — Job 22:27–28

Step (Repeat 10 times):
“Let light be.” — Genesis 1:3

Decade 4: I Call Things Into Being

Will Declaration:
“God gives names to things which have no being, as if they were.” — Romans 4:17

Step (Repeat 10 times):
“You will make the decision, and it will be done for you.” — Job 22:28

Decade 5: I Rest in Fulfilment

Will Declaration:
“It is done as you have said.” — Matthew 8:13

Step (Repeat 10 times):
“In quietness and in confidence will be your strength.” — Isaiah 30:15

Closing Benediction: The Quiet Reign of Completion

“I have overcome the world.” — John 16:33
“All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me.” — Matthew 28:18
“Let it be to me as you say.” — Luke 1:38
