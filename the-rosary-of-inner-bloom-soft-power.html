The Rosary of Inner Bloom: Soft Power and Divine Desire

A Neville Goddard-Inspired Devotional in Soft Power and Divine Desire

This is not a prayer of striving. It is a gentle return to what already is.
It is the slow flowering of your heart’s desire,
curated to embody softness, receptivity, and the quiet confidence of assumption.

Opening Invocation: The Divine Whisper of Identity

“My loved one is mine, and I am his.” — Song of Songs 2:16
Begin here: in belovedness.
All movement begins in union, not in distance.

First Bead: Open to Receive

“Let your request be made known to God.” — Philippians 4:6
Desire is not a flaw. It is the holy spark.
You may ask—and trust.

The Next Three Beads: The Gentle Renewal of Mind

“Take your rest in the Lord, waiting quietly for him” — Psalm 37:7
Waiting is not weakness. It is sacred magnetism.

“I will make her come into the waste land and say words of comfort to her heart.” — Hosea 2:14
Even in barrenness, he speaks comfort.

“The Lord is your keeper; the Lord is your shade on your right hand.” — Psalm 121:5
You are protected, always—even from the heat of your own doubts.

Before the Decades: Establish the Inner Garden

“The smell of your sweet perfumes is better than all spices.” — Song of Songs 4:10
You are already fragrant with promise.
You do not need to bloom to be beautiful.

Five Decades: Petals of Fulfilled Desire

Each decade begins with a Desire Declaration, followed by a Petal Verse to be softly repeated ten times.
This is the rhythm of faith—not effort, but expectancy.

1. I Am Heard and Held

Desire Declaration:
“You will make your prayer to him, and he will give ear to you; and you will give effect to your oaths.” — Job 22:27

Petal Verse (repeat 10 times):
“The Lord is near all those who give honour to his name.” — Psalm 145:18

2. I Am Already Becoming

Desire Declaration:
“The upright will see his face.” — Psalm 11:7

Petal Verse (repeat 10 times):
“He will make your righteousness be seen like the light, and your cause like the shining of the sun.” — Psalm 37:6

3. I Am the Hidden Treasure

Desire Declaration:
“The secret things are the Lord our God's.” — Deuteronomy 29:29

Petal Verse (repeat 10 times):
“The kingdom of heaven is like a secret store of wealth in a field.” — Matthew 13:44

4. I Am the Anointed One

Desire Declaration:
“You have made me glad with the oil of joy.” — Psalm 45:7

Petal Verse (repeat 10 times):
“You put a crown of honour on my head.” — Psalm 21:3

5. I Am the Desired Fulfilment

Desire Declaration:
“You will be a crown of glory in the hand of the Lord, a king's head-dress in the hand of your God.” — Isaiah 62:3

Petal Verse (repeat 10 times):
“And they will give him the name Immanuel; which is, God with us.” — Matthew 1:23

Closing Benediction: Return to Stillness, Rooted in Promise

“In peace I will take my rest and sleep; for you only, Lord, keep me safe.” — Psalm 4:8
“The Lord will give strength to his people; the Lord will give his people the blessing of peace.” — Psalm 29:11
“You will keep him in perfect peace whose mind is fixed on you.” — Isaiah 26:3

Feel the petals closing. The fragrance lingers.
Your desire is heard.
Your heart is the sacred ground.
