About the Author

Author Background

HNNH is a researcher and writer focused on the psychological and analytical study of Scripture. Drawing deeply from the teachings of Neville Goddard, HNNH explores biblical narratives not as historical events, but as a mental roadmap of human consciousness and the creative power of imagination.

A Rational Approach (Non-Traditional)

Unlike traditional theology or religious dogma, HNNH’s work approaches the Bible from a rational, psychological stance. The focus is on the Law of Assumption—the understanding that the "God" of scripture is a personification of the human imagination as the Judges and Rulers of Self. This is an analytical study of mind-mechanics, not a religious or denominational interpretation.

Commitment to Clarity

HNNH is dedicated to preserving the integrity of Neville’s insights while maintaining a high standard of interpretive care. By focusing on inner mechanics rather than outward ritual, this work provides a grounded, trustworthy resource for those exploring the intersection of thought and manifestation.
