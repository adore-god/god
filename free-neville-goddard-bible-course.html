Free Self-Guided Course in Understanding Scripture

Discover the Bible in a new way with god.thway.uk, a free self-guided course designed to teach you the key principles of reading and understanding scripture. This pathway is perfect for anyone curious about the deeper meaning of the Bible and how it can be applied to your life.

Neville Goddard taught audiences who were eager to learn directly from the Bible. He showed that scripture is not a historical or religious text, but a sophisticated guide to imagination, consciousness, and manifestation. This website follows that approach, offering fundamental principles first, and then connecting you to cross-linked series collections for deeper exploration of different sections of the Bible.

Start with the Fundamentals

The free self-guided course begins in the right hand sidebar menu with the essential principles you need to unlock scripture:

  • Understanding the Bible as a guide to consciousness and imagination.

  • Recognising the symbolic meaning behind biblical characters, events, and promises.

  • Learning how faith and assumption turn unseen possibilities into lived reality.

Once you’ve grasped the fundamentals, you can explore the series collections, each linked to specific Bible passages and themes. These collections offer clear explanations and practical insights, helping you connect scripture to everyday life.

A Free, Flexible Learning Experience

This course is completely free and self-paced. There are no sign-ups, no deadlines — you decide how and when to explore the material. By starting with the fundamentals and moving into the collections, you’ll gain the same understanding Neville taught to his audience: how to read the Bible as a practical guide to imagination and the creative power of consciousness.

Why This Free Course Can Transform Your Reading of the Bible

Many people read the Bible superficially, missing the symbolic and practical lessons embedded in its stories. By following this self-guided course, you will:

  • See the Bible as a living guide, not just a historical document.

  • Understand how imagination and faith shape your reality.

  • Apply the lessons of scripture to consciously manifest your desires.

Begin Your Journey Today

Start with the fundamental principles, then explore the series collections to deepen your understanding. This free self-guided course is your roadmap to reading the Bible as Neville’s audience did — as a dynamic, practical guide to personal growth, imagination, and the power within you.
