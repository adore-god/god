Did you know that the Bible, in itself, is not a religious or historical text, but a profound, psychological, and self-contained work? It teaches that imagination is God, and shows how to shape reality through faith, love, and assumption. Its practical narrative is rooted in a few foundational verses in Genesis, through which Neville Goddard revealed the Bible as the law of assumption.
