Rosary of Heavenly Wings: Aligned with the Power of Desire and Assumption

Curated to embody softness, receptivity, and the quiet confidence of assumption.

Theme: Wings and Angels – Feminine, Desire-Based, BBE Version

Opening (Introductory Invocation)

At the Cross: Identity
“I made you and gave you strength, and I will be your helper.” — Isaiah 41:10

I am held aloft in the embrace of the Invisible. My name is whispered on wings unseen. I am what I am—resting in the assurance that I do not carry myself. I am carried.

First Bead: Receiving

“Are they not all spirits who take news, sent out to be servants to those whose heritage will be salvation?” — Hebrews 1:14

I receive what is mine by divine message and ministering grace. All that is mine comes to me with wings. The messengers are already dispatched.

Next Three Beads (Prepare the Soil)

“He will give you the wings of a dove covered with silver, its feathers shining with gold.” — Psalm 68:13
“You have been kept safe under the wings of the Most High.” — Psalm 91:4
“Let the word of Christ be in you in all wisdom.” — Colossians 3:16

I am a garden enclosed, kissed by winged revelation. I prepare the soil of my heart with softness, stillness, and song.

Before First Decade (Set the Mood)

“He will give his angels orders about you, to keep you in all your ways.” — Psalm 91:11

The air around me is thick with wings. I walk in the softness of divine supervision. My ways are not watched—they are witnessed. I am held and seen.

Five Decades: The Soft Power of Desire

Each decade includes a central verse (Desire Declaration) and ten affirming verses (Petals), drawing out the feminine themes of receptivity, faith, incubation, and fulfilment.

Decade 1: I Am Carried on Wings

Desire Declaration:
“You have seen what I did... how I took you on eagle’s wings and took you to myself.” — Exodus 19:4

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“He will keep you from falling, and will lift you up with joy.” — Jude 1:24

Decade 2: I Am the Message Fulfilled

Desire Declaration:
“The word which goes out of my mouth will not come back to me without effect.” — Isaiah 55:11

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“Blessed is the woman who believed what the Lord said to her would be done.” — Luke 1:45

Decade 3: I Am Under the Shadow of Wings

Desire Declaration:
“Let me be safe under the shade of your wings.” — Psalm 17:8

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“The Lord will cover you with his feathers, and under his wings you will be safe.” — Psalm 91:4

Decade 4: I Am the Open Heaven

Desire Declaration:
“I saw heaven opening, and a ladder reaching to the earth, with angels going up and down on it.” — Genesis 28:12

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“The angels of God are always before the face of the Father.” — Matthew 18:10

Decade 5: I Am Lifted and Sent

Desire Declaration:
“Then the spirit took me up, and I heard the sound of great wings.” — Ezekiel 3:12

Petal (Repeat 10 times):
“Go in peace; the desire of your heart is granted to you.” — 1 Samuel 1:17

Closing Benediction: The Quiet Flowering of the Wish

“He will give his angels orders to take care of you.” — Luke 4:10
“Let my prayer come before you like sweet perfume, and the lifting up of my hands like the evening offering.” — Psalm 141:2
“He has put a new song in my mouth.” — Psalm 40:3

My prayer does not rise—it is already risen. I do not wait to be lifted—I am the lifting. Desire has wings, and I rest beneath them.
