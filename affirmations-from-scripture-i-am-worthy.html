Affirmations from Scripture for the Woman Who Remembers Her Power

This collection of Bible-based affirmations is for the one who no longer begs, but believes — the one who knows that desire is sacred, and fulfilment is her birthright. Each verse, taken from the Bible in Basic English (BBE), has been gently shaped into a first-person declaration of truth. Speak them aloud. Whisper them in the quiet. Let them shape your inner world, for what you accept as true within will appear without

1. I am what I am.

“And God said to Moses, I am what I am.”
— Exodus 3:14

2. I am still, and I know that I am divine.

“Be still, and see that I am God.”
— Psalm 46:10

3. The Lord takes pleasure in my well-being.

“Let them give cries of joy… The Lord has pleasure in the well-being of his servant.”
— Psalm 35:27

4. My desires are safe in the hands of God.

“So be glad in the Lord; and let your heart be full of joy; and he will give you the desires of your heart.”
— Psalm 37:4

5. I believe I have received it, and it is mine.

“Whatever you make a request for in prayer, have faith that it has been given to you, and you will have it.”
— Mark 11:24

6. As I have faith, it is done to me.

“As you have had faith, so let it be done to you.”
— Matthew 8:13

7. I am not afraid — I am made perfect in love.

“There is no fear in love: true love has no room for fear, because where fear is, there is pain; and he who is not free from fear is not complete in love.”
— 1 John 4:18

8. No good thing is withheld from me.

“The Lord gives grace and glory: he will not keep back any good thing from those whose ways are upright.”
— Psalm 84:11

9. I have completed the inner work — now I rest.

“I have given you glory on the earth, having done the work which you gave me to do.”
— John 17:4

10. I call things not yet seen as though they are.

“God… gives life to the dead, and gives names to things which have no being, as if they were.”
— Romans 4:17

11. As He is, so am I — radiant, whole, and present.

“As he is, so are we in this world.”
— 1 John 4:17

12. All things are freely given to me.

“He who did not keep back his only Son, but gave him up for us all, will he not with him freely give us all things?”
— Romans 8:32

13. The Lord is my provider; I do not lack.

“The Lord takes care of me as his sheep; I will not be without any good thing.”
— Psalm 23:1

14. My life is crowned with loving-kindness.

“He crowns you with loving-kindness and tender mercies.”
— Psalm 103:4

15. I dwell in peace, without fear of harm.

“You will be safe in your resting-place, and nothing will take away your sleep.”
— Proverbs 3:24

16. I am chosen, called, and beloved.

“You did not take me for yourselves, but I took you for myself… that your fruit may be lasting.”
— John 15:16
